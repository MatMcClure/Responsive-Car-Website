Agility

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ has a 6.5 liter V12 engine that produces 770 horsepower and 531 pound-feet of torque.

Performance

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ can go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in 2.5 seconds and can run a quarter mile in 10.3 seconds. It has a top speed of over 219 miles per hour.
